Presentation

In Depth is a dungeon game in voxel art with a humor present in the different bosses of the dungeon. In Depth was born with a very strong inspiration from dungeon game but also geek and pop culture humor.

  • Browse the dungeon with the different adventurers and face creatures and bosses all different from each other.
  • Upgrade your gear and skills to be virtually invincible.
  • Ask the other adventurers in the tavern for help in exchange for a quest.
  • Fight with several adventurers thanks to multiplayer.(Not currently available)
