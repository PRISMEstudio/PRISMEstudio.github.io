Usage Warning

At the request of the decree of November 2, 1994 ordering the posting of warnings on the packaging and instructions for use of video games as well as in establishments making video games available to the public by French law .


Read this before using a video game by yourself or your child.

Some people are susceptible to epileptic seizures or to fainting when they see certain types of flashing lights or things that are common in our daily environment. These people are at risk of seizures when they watch certain television images or when they play certain video games. These phenomena can appear even though the subject has no medical history or has never been confronted with an epileptic seizure.

If you or a member of your family have already experienced symptoms related to epilepsy (seizure or loss of consciousness) in the presence of light stimulation, please consult your doctor before use.

We advise parents to be mindful of their children when playing video games. If you or your child experiences any of the following symptoms: dizziness, blurred vision, twitching of the eyes or muscles, loss of consciousness, orientation disturbances, involuntary movement or seizures, please stop playing immediately and seek medical advice. doctor.


Precautions to be taken in all cases when using a video game:
- When using a video game connectable to a screen, play a safe distance away from that television screen and as far as the connection cord allows.
- Preferably use video games on a small screen.
- Avoid playing if you are tired or lack sleep.
- Make sure you play in a well-lit room.

In use, take ten to fifteen minute breaks every hour.

How to download and install "In Depth" ?

To download and install the PRISME Launcher for "In Depth", then please follow the instructions below.


1- Download the latest version using the download button on the download page from "In Depth" ( here ).

2- Once downloaded, open the file (named "PRISMELauncher_InDepth_Setup_Win").

3-Once opened, select the language of the installer. Then click on "Next".

4- Continue the installation by carefully reading the usage warnings (you can find them here ) as well as the terms of use ( here ).

5- You have installed the basic launcher files now you can run it, it will update itself automatically, once the update is done it starts downloading and installing the game.

6- Wait for the download and installation time, when it has finished a "Play" button appears.

Here your game is installed and ready to play, it will be kept up to the last version thanks to the PRISME Launcher.
If you have any problem do not hesitate to contact us via the heading "Contact us", all this information will of course be kept confidential.
