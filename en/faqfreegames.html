How do I access announcements from the #FreeGames service?

Go to the website https://prismestudio.github.io/freegames and click on the "Join us >" button.
Accept the invitation, and here you are!

You only have two last little details to settle: first select your language in the #welcome channel, then go for a little walk in the #rules channel.

How can I be notified of the games offered?

In the #rules channel, look for the "Role assignments" section (the last one normally ;) )

Then react to the message with the emoji corresponding to your preferences!

How do I put the adverts on my discord server?

To put the ads on your server, please follow the instructions below.

1- Go to #free-games channel.

2- On PC: At the top of the window, next to the channel title, click on the "Follow" button.

On mobile: At the bottom of the screen, click on the "Follow" button.


3- Select the server and the channel where you want to add the announcements.

And here you go, come in this channel for the next offer.

If you have a problem, do not hesitate to contact us via the "Contact us" section by putting your discord account in the message (username#0000), all this information will be treated according to our privacy policy.

How do I invite friends to #FreeGames?

It's easy !

Share him this link:
https://prismestudio.github.io/freegames

All he has to do is click on the "Join us >" button and then accept the invitation on Discord. And tadaaaa !

How to participate in a giveaway?

If a Giveaway has been announced, go to the start date of the giveaway in the #giveaway channel which will appear at the top of channel list.

Read the conditions of participation as well as the rules (available in the section "Conditions of participation and rules for giveaways/contests" just below).

Then simply react with the :tada: emoji under the message from GiveawayBot#2381.

Conditions of participation and rules for giveaways/contests

I.Conditions of participation
You must meet to the following criteria:

  • - Be at least 16 years old.
  • - Have a Discord account with the "Allow private messages from server members" setting enabled in order to be contacted ONLY by L'Ogre#4527 in case of victory.
    (Right click on the server > Privacy setting)
  • - An account on the platform corresponding to the giveaway, in order to claim your reward.
  • - Read the Rules available below

II.Rules
Please read the document below carefully:
Giveaways/contests rules 12/25/2021(pdf)

What is the beta tester program and how do I participate?

The beta tester program is a program that helps us to test more or less experimental functionalities on a larger scale.
For example, at the moment we are experimenting with email notifications.
Later we would like to test a dashboard with accounts linked to your Discord account.

Want to help us ?
/!\ We limit the number of places available for the program.

To join us follow the steps below:
1- Go to https://prismestudio.github.io/en/contactus
2- Enter your email address (this is also the one that will be used for notifications)
3- Select "Beta tester program"
4- Read the privacy policy then check the two boxes below, and finally enter your Discord account (example: username#0000)
5- Click on "Send"
6- You will then receive an email confirming that you have been added to the beta!
