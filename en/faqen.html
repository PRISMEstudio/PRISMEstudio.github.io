Discord community server rules

You must respect the rules below, under penalty of being banned from the server.

1 - Respect the member of PRISME Studio, Administrator, and the other member of the server.
2 - Racist, anti-Semitic, handiphobic, sexist or homophobic insults are strongly prohibited.
3 - Respect the rules in the description of a channel.
4 - Of course you can put some of announcment channel in your server (for exemple #news or #patch-note).
5 - Don't make auto-promotion/promotion for a discord server without the agreement of an Administrator or a member of PRISME Studio.
6 - Before participate at a giveaway hosted on the Discord server of PRISME Studio please check the rules of the giveaway to see if you are eligible to receive the reward.
7 - If you have a question or a request for PRISME Studio, don't use discord, go here .

Tester rules

You must respect the rules below, under penalty of being banned from the tester program.

/!\ Warning: These rules may change depending on the game.

If the game/application is at the stage of Development
1 - It is forbidden to produce content (image, video, stream and other) with it.
2 - Don't share the game/application with your freinds or other.
3 - Don't share the code with your freinds or other.
4 - Keep this sercret just for yourself.

If the game/application is at the stage of Alpha
1 - It is forbidden to produce content (image, video, stream and other) with it.
2 - Don't share the game/application with your freinds or other.
3 - Don't share the code with your freinds or other.

If the game/application is at the stage of Beta
1 - You can produce content (image, video, stream and other) with it.
2 - Don't share the game/application with your freinds or other.
3 - Don't share the code with your freinds or other.

Please, if you can, always report bug or error you encounter here .
