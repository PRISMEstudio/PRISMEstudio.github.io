Conditions of participation in giveaways

You simply need to meet the following criteria:

  • - Have a discord account with the parameter "Allow private messages from members of the server" activated.
    Right click on the server > Privacy parameter
  • - An account on the corresponding platform of the giveaway, in order to claim your reward.
  • - Read the rules below.

How do I access to free games announcements?

Go to the website of #Jeux Gratuits and click the "Connect" button on the discord widget.
When you are in the server, select your language in #welcome.

How do I put the adverts on my discord server?

To put the ads on your server, please follow the instructions below.

1- Go to #free-games channel.

2- At the top of the window, next to the channel title, click on the "Follow" button.


3- Select the server and the channel where you want to add the announcements.

If you have a problem, do not hesitate to contact us via the section "Contact Us" by putting your discord account (nickname#0000) in the message, all this information will be of course kept confidential.
